About Eddie

Eddie is an AI-powered assistant created to support orthodontic professionals in their daily work.

By automating repetitive tasks such as drafting clinical reports, organizing patient data, and creating checklists, Eddie helps you focus on what matters most — delivering excellent care to your patients.

Designed with simplicity and security in mind, Eddie combines intuitive technology with strict data protection standards to make your workflow smoother, smarter, and safer.
